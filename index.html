About Me

Hi, I'm Charles, a postgraduate student currently pursuing a Master of Laws (LLM) at the University of Warwick. My academic journey focuses on international business law, with key modules including The Regulation of Mergers & Acquisitions, International Banking & Securities Regulation, and International Corporate Governance & Financial Regulation.

Prior to my postgraduate studies, I completed a Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Politics with Quantitative Methods at Queen's University Belfast. My undergraduate studies allowed me to explore various political frameworks, with particular interest in Security & Terrorism, Political Parties & Elections in Northern Ireland, and World Politics.

I've created this blog to share my thoughts, experiences, and opinions on a range of topics and current events. If you're reading this, thank you for visiting, and please feel free to share your perspectives with me.
